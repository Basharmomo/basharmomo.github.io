Recipe #1: Chocolate Chip cookies

Comments From Real People:

This is such an easy chocolate chip cookie. No special equipment, no creaming -- a perfect cookie to do with kids. We love how versatile this dough is, too. It makes an awesome rocky road bar cookie.

Time:

  • prep: 20mins
  • inactive: 30mins
  • cook: 15mins
Ingredients Quantity
Unsalted Butter 1/2 cup (1 stick)
packed dark brown sugar 3/4 cup
sugar 3/4 cup
large eggs 2
pure vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
semisweet chocolate chips, or chunks 1 (12-ounce) bag
all-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
baking soda 3/4 teaspoon
fine salt 1 teaspoon

Steps:

Step 1: Evenly position 2 racks in the middle of the oven and preheat to 375 degrees F. (on convection
setting if you have it.)
Step 2:Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone sheets. (If you only have 1 baking sheet,
let it cool completely between batches.)
Step 3: Put the butter in a microwave safe bowl, cover and microwave on medium power until melted.
(Alternatively melt in a small saucepan.) Cool slightly.
Step 4: Whisk the sugars, eggs, butter and vanilla in a large bowl until smooth.
Step 5:Whisk the flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl. Stir the dry ingredients
into the wet ingredients with a wooden spoon; take care not to over mix. Stir in the chocolate chips or
chunks.
Step 6:Scoop heaping tablespoons of the dough onto the prepared pans. Wet hands slightly and roll the
dough into balls.
Step 7:Space the cookies about 2-inches apart on the pans.
Step 8:Bake, until golden, but still soft in the center, 12 to 16 minutes, depending on how chewy or
crunchy you like your cookies.
Step 9: Transfer hot cookies with a spatula to a rack to cool. Serve!!!.

Recipe #2: Macaroni and Cheese

Comments From real people:

Made this few times now. My favorite. Not real greasy like others I've tried. I always add cut up smoked ham too. Reminds me of family recipe.

Tme:

  • prep: 15mins
  • cook: 40mins
Ingrediants Quantity
Dried macaroni 4 cups
Whole egg 1
Butter 1/2 stick (4 tablespoons)
All-purpose flour 1/4 cup
Whole milk 2 1/2 cups
Dry mustard 2 heaping teaspoons (more if desired)
Sharp Cheddar, grated (not pre-grated cheese) 1 pound, plus more for baking
Salt As desired
Seasoned salt As desired
Ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Optional spices: cayenne pepper, paprika, thyme As desired

Steps:

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Step 2:Cook the macaroni until still slightly firm. Drain and set aside.
Step 3:In a small bowl, beat the egg.
Step 4:In a large pot, melt the butter and sprinkle in the flour. Whisk together over medium-low heat.
Cook for a couple of minutes, whisking constantly. Don't let it burn.
Step 5: Pour in the milk, add the mustard and whisk until smooth. Cook until very thick, about 5 minutes.
Step 6: Reduce the heat to low. Take 1/4 cup of the sauce and slowly pour it into the beaten egg, whisking constantly to avoid cooking the eggs.
Step 7:Whisk together until smooth. Pour the egg into the sauce, whisking constantly. Stir until smooth.
Step 8: Add in the cheese and stir to melt. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt and the pepper.
Step 9: Add any additional spices if desired. Taste the sauce and add more salt and seasoned salt as needed! DO NOT UNDERSALT.
Step 10:Pour in the drained, cooked macaroni and stir to combine.
Step 11:Serve immediately (while it's still very creamy) or pour into a buttered baking dish, top with extra cheese and bake until bubbly and golden on top, 20 to 25 minutes.

Recipe: Guacamole #3

Comments From Real People:

My family LOVES this. There is never any leftovers when this is made!

Time:

  • Total: 15mins
Ingrediants Quantity
ripe avocados 4
juiced lime 1
red onion, chopped 1/2
garlic clove, minced 1
tomato, diced 1
fresh cilantro, finely chopped 2 tablespoons
cumin 1/4 teaspoon
chili powder 1/4 teaspoon
Salt and pepper As desired

Steps

Step 1: Pit and halve avocados and scoop out flesh into a mixing bowl.
Step 2: Mash avocados with a fork, leaving it still a bit chunky.
Step 3:Add the juiced lime, red onion; chopped, garlic clovel; minced, tomato; diced, fresh cilantro; finely chopped, cumin, chili powder, salt, pepper, and mix gently.
